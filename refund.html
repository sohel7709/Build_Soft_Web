Our Policy

At BuildSoft IT Solutions, we are committed to delivering high-quality services that meet our clients' expectations. Due to the nature of our digital products and services, we maintain a no-refund policy once services have been delivered or digital products have been accessed.

No refunds will be provided for:

  • Completed web development projects
  • Digital marketing services already rendered
  • Custom software development
  • Consultation services that have been delivered
  • Digital products that have been downloaded or accessed

For ongoing services or subscription-based products, cancellations must be made before the next billing cycle to avoid charges. Please contact our support team if you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy.
